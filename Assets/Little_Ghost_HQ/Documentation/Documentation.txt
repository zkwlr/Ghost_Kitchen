	
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - Little Ghost(PBR-HQ)- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - 

This pack contains 3d Model and Animation's of a cute Little Ghost, that can used in any type of your games.
This is a high quality version of the previous package named "Little Ghost low poly".This pack offers, highly detailed model's 
with 2K PBR textures in it.You can use both packages based on your game theme & performance.

Character & Props details:

- Male ghost : 3 variation's(White ghost, Bloody ghost & Muddy ghost) 
- Female ghost : 2 variation's
- Axe(Iron & Golden Axe)
- Devil Head
- Witch hat : Gives your ghost a magical look. Pink and Blue colors(Clean and blooded)


Technical Details:-
				
		- Vertex count : Male ghost(1136), Female ghost(1178), Axe(600), Hat(884), Devil head(254)
		- Tris count :  Male ghost(2268), Femal ghost(2352), Axe(1196), Hat(1764), Devil head(476)
		- Animations : 11 (Idle, Run/Fly, Attack, Attack-2(with Axe), Look, Hide, Wake up, Hit, Dance1, Dance2, Playing)
		- Texture Variations : 3(Male ghost), 3(Props), 2(Female ghost)
		- Used Material(s) : Unity Standard Shader
		- Texture maps : Albedo/Base Color
		- Texture Size : 2048(.png)
		- Simple colliders attached

Features:-
	- Customizable character's
	- PBR HQ Textures
	- Many cute animations included
	- Include many shape, color variations
	- Props with multiple textures included
	- Smooth animations	
	- Mobile, AR supported

Folder details:-

	Materials : Where the materials for the model located.
	
	Meshes : Contain FBX format of the 3d model.
	
	Textures : Contains Textures, that are in (.PNG) file format
	
	(Other folders - Prefabs, Scenes, Animations)
Notes:

Support URP, HDRP, LWRP, Build-in Render Pipeline
This project is submitted using Built-in Render Pipeline. If you are not using Built-in pipeline, you need to manually reset the shader of the model to avoid it being pink

How to use:-
	
	Simply drag and drop the prefab to your scene and use it.
	(Prefab(s) located under 
	Asset > Little Ghost HQ > Prefabs).
